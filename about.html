About Us

Pinas Sarap is an informational and educational website dedicated to promoting Filipino cuisine and cultural heritage. The platform highlights famous dishes from the three major island groups of the Philippines—Luzon, Visayas, and Mindanao—to showcase the country's rich culinary diversity.

Our website was created to address the limited global representation and accessible online resources about Filipino food and culture. Beyond sharing recipes, Pinas Sarap provides information about the history, origin, and cultural significance of each dish, helping users better understand the traditions behind Filipino cuisine.

Vision

To be a reliable and accessible online platform that promotes Filipino cuisine and cultural heritage to both local and global audiences, inspiring appreciation and understanding of the Philippines' diverse culinary traditions.

Mission

  • Highlight representative dishes from Luzon, Visayas, and Mindanao
  • Provide information about preparation and cultural significance
  • Create an educational resource for students and tourists
  • Promote appreciation of Filipino cuisine
