Binakol

Binakol is a Filipino chicken soup cooked with coconut water, ginger, lemongrass, and vegetables. It comes from the Visayas and is known for its light yet flavorful broth and aromatic finish.

Traditionally prepared in bamboo tubes, binakol captures the rich sweetness of coconut water while keeping the dish warm and comforting. It is often enjoyed on rainy days and family gatherings.

How to Cook

  1. Sauté aromatics:

    In a pot, sauté ginger and lemongrass until fragrant. Add chicken pieces and brown lightly.


  2. Add liquids:

    Pour in coconut water and fish sauce. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer until the chicken is tender.


  3. Add vegetables:

    Add sliced green papaya or chayote and cook until crisp-tender. Stir in coconut milk near the end.


  4. Finish and serve:

    Sprinkle chopped green onions over the soup. Serve hot with steamed rice.
